## Brand & Voice Guidelines

### Brand: AI Coaching Academy / Caelan Huntress

**Voice characteristics:**
- **Direct and practical** (not academic or overly technical)
- **Playful productivity** (approachable, not corporate-stiff)
- **Results-focused** (emphasize outcomes over features)
- **Conversational** (like talking to a smart colleague, not reading marketing copy)

**Avoid:**
- Jargon and buzzwords ("synergy," "leverage," "transformation")
- Hype and exaggeration ("revolutionary," "game-changing")
- Corporate-speak ("solutions provider," "best-in-class")
- Fear-mongering ("or your competitors will destroy you")

**Use:**
- Specific numbers (hours saved, dollar values)
- Real examples and scenarios
- Plain language explanations
- Gentle humor where appropriate

## Design Style

### Overall Aesthetic
- **Clean and spacious** (generous whitespace, not cramped)
- **Professional but approachable** (not corporate-stiff, not startup-casual)
- **Scan-friendly** (easy to skim, clear hierarchy)
- **Bold typography** (League Spartan makes strong statements)
- **Classic with modern touches** (Libre Baskerville adds sophistication)

### Layout
- **Single column on mobile** (responsive, mobile-first)
- **Max-width container** (1200px) for readability on desktop
- **Generous padding** on all sections (80-120px vertical spacing)

### Visual Elements
- **Icons:** Simple line icons for checkmarks, features (Kelly Green for positive elements)
- **Dividers:** Subtle horizontal rules in Platinum (#EFF2F1) or thin Cobalt Blue lines
- **Cards:** Subtle shadows or borders for testimonials, deliverables (white cards on Platinum backgrounds)
- **Accents:** Mustard Yellow for badges, callouts, highlights (use sparingly)
- **No heavy graphics:** Keep it clean, let typography and color do the work

### Components to Include
- **Testimonial cards** (quote in Libre Baskerville + name in Futura Medium + optional Mustard Yellow accent)
- **Feature grid** (What You Receive section as 2-column grid with Kelly Green checkmarks)
- **Comparison table** (Three paths forward as simple table or cards)
- **FAQ accordion** (collapsible questions with Cobalt Blue headers)
- **Sticky header** (optional, with Orange Red CTA button)

## Design Inspiration

- Bold headlines with strong hierarchy

- Generic agency templates
- Busy, cluttered layouts
- Aggressive sales tactics
