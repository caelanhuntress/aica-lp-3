Frequently Asked Questions

Can we do the workshop without committing to ongoing work?

Absolutely. The workshop is designed to deliver standalone value. You'll walk away with a complete, polished AI Roadmap — whether or not you choose to continue working together afterwards.

What happens after the workshop?

You choose from three paths: self-guided implementation using your roadmap, ongoing AI coaching for monthly support, or done-with-you consulting for hands-on help. There's no pressure to commit to anything beyond the workshop itself.

How many people should attend?

5-15 participants is optimal. Include a mix of decision-makers (owners, directors), department managers who understand operations, and team leads who know the daily workflows. The best results come from having diverse perspectives in the room.

Is this only for technical teams?

Not at all. No technical background is required. The workshop is designed for business people — managers, directors, and owners. We focus on business outcomes, not technical implementation details.

What if we have strict data security requirements?

We work within your constraints. The roadmap will account for your security policies and recommend enterprise-grade AI tools that meet your compliance requirements. Data security is always part of the conversation.
